Charles Darwin

A short biography by Logan Lowery

Who Was He

Charles Darwin was a famous scientist born in 1809 in England, and is very known for his theory of evolution by nateral selection.

Early Life

Charles Darwin was very interested in nature at a young age, and went on to study at Cambridge's University from 1828 to 1831, which is where he discovered his passion for natural science, but it was his five year voyage on HMS Beagle were Darwin was established as an eminent geologist, it was the voyage where he developed his major theories through observations made.

His Theory

In 1859, he wrote a book called On the Orgin of Species it explained how animals would have changed over a time though a process called Nateral Selection

Main points to his thoery

  • Variation: Individuals of a species are not identical.
  • Inheritance: Traits are passed from generation to generation.
  • Struggle for existence: More offspring are born than can survive.

Why He Matters

Charles Darwin changed how people think about life, and science. His ideas are still importand today in Biology, abnd other sciences.
